Pokemon GO Wiki

Pokemon GO is a popular new game created by Niantic Studios. The game is focused on players moving around in real life in order to move a character on the screen. The character then attempts to catch pokemon and find Pokestops and Gyms, special locations in the world.

Reasons that Pokemon Go is such a sucess.

  • It's fun to walk around town.
  • It's a great game to play with friends, and you can battle other people.
  • It still is a lot like the orginal Pokemon games, meaning that poeple can still relate to the game.
  • The game is really interactive, and changes due to the user's location.
  • You can join a team, either Team Valor, Team Mystic, or Team Instinct.
  • When you find a gym that your team controls, you can add one of your pokemon to the gym for others to fight.
